Instructions
1. Generate a new paper wallet by clicking on the "Generate" button. Download the zip file below and run index.html on an offline computer for maximum security.
2. Print your new wallet by clicking on the "Print" button. It is highly recommended to print multiple copies and store seperately in secure locations.
3. Public Address is used to send and receive funds. Check your balance and transaction history by going to etherscan.io and entering your public address.
4. Private Key is used to sign and verify transactions. Do not share your private key with anyone or your funds will be stolen.
5. Seed Phrase is used to recover your wallet in case you lose access. Many wallet providers support importing a wallet through its seed phrase. Do not share your private key with anyone or your funds will be stolen.
See Github repository for more information. (Download zip).
